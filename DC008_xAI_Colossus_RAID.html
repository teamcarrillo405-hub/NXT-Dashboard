Investment Thesis

xAI Colossus represents the fastest AI infrastructure buildout in history. Elon Musk's AI company went from site selection (March 2024) to 100,000 H100 GPUs operational (September 3, 2024) in just 122 days, a pace 2.5x faster than industry norms. By December 2024, the cluster expanded to 230K+ GPUs (150K H100s, 50K H200s, 30K GB200s). Colossus 2 broke ground in March 2025 with an $80M land acquisition and targets 200 MW additional capacity by August 2025. Total capital raised exceeds $32B including a $20B Series E in January 2026 at $230B valuation. Investors include QIA, NVIDIA, Cisco, BlackRock, Morgan Stanley, and Kingdom Holdings. The company operates 35+ gas turbines (421 MW combined) with contested air permits. SELC and NAACP filed EPA complaints citing Clean Air Act violations. Despite regulatory friction, execution velocity remains exceptional with priority NVIDIA GPU access and strategic partnerships with Dell and Supermicro. (Sources: xAI Series E announcement Jan 2026; TIME Dec 2025; Data Center Dynamics; SELC filings)
